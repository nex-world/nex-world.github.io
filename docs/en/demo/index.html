NEXWORLD Demo

Try the NEXWORLD Demo

This demo shows how NEXWORLD uses AI to drive a dynamic text adventure world. Your choices are not just menu selections; they become part of the story state and can change what happens next.

Story Setup

You wake up in a xianxia-inspired world as Yue Yanyan, the arrogant daughter of River City’s lord. Thanks to your family name and natural talent, you’ve always been outspoken and proud. But just now, a flood of unfamiliar memories surges into your mind — you realize with horror that you are a side character in a novel, a villain fated to be killed by the protagonist in a battle meant to showcase their power.

Time is running out. The duel that decides your fate is only three days away.

Will you accept your destiny, or use this “foreknowledge” to try rewriting the story? How will this world respond to your struggle?

Start your three-day survival journey

Important note: The demo requires an AI model to run. On first use, follow the on-screen instructions and enter a valid API Key in the “Model Configuration” section at the bottom. If you have any questions, feel free to join the player community below.

Also Try: Tavern AI Roleplay Platform

If the demo sparked your interest in interacting with an AI world, and you want more freedom to create your own characters and stories, we have another tool for you:

Tavern is a lightweight, easy-to-use AI roleplay platform. Unlike the story-driven demo, Tavern gives you full creative control.

In Tavern, you can:

  • Create any character: From fantasy heroes to modern idols, define their appearance, personality, and backstory in detail.
  • Experience multiple play modes:
    • Free Chat: Have open-ended conversations with characters.
    • Challenge Mode: Set goals (e.g., “Earn their trust”) and complete challenges through dialogue.
    • DnD Mode: Experience full tabletop roleplaying rules with character attributes and dice for adventures.
    • Group Chat Mode: Bring multiple AI characters into one conversation and watch their lively interactions.
  • Full control: All conversation history and character data is stored on your local device, managed entirely by you.

Tavern is the perfect sandbox for your imagination to explore the diverse乐趣 of AI roleplay.
