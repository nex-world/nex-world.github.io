Life Restart

Life Restart Simulator AI

An AI-powered life simulation where every life is a unique story.

Life Restart Simulator AI is our creative adaptation of the classic text-based life game. Built on the original random-event framework, we integrate large language model (LLM) technology to replace preset text libraries with intelligent, dynamic storytelling.

Every time you click “Restart”, you are not just rolling stats — you are beginning a rich biographical novel written by AI in real time.

Key Features

  • 🤖 AI-Driven Dynamic Narrative No more fixed text combinations. Every stage of your life — childhood adventures, school days, career turns, relationships — is generated in real time by AI based on your attributes, ensuring each playthrough is unique.

  • 🎲 High Randomness with Deep Connections Your starting talents and choices ripple through your entire life like a butterfly effect. The AI doesn’t just generate isolated events — it understands causality and emotional connections to build a coherent, meaningful life story.

  • 📝 Rich Text Experience From concise life snapshots to vivid scene descriptions, AI paints every important moment of your life journey, delivering a complete narrative spanning grand destiny to intimate emotion.

  • 🌟 Explore Infinite Possibilities From ordinary to legendary, from scientist to artist, discover the countless paths a life can take under the influence of complex variables. Each restart is a new exploration of what life could be.
