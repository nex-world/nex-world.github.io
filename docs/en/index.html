NexTavern: Lightweight AI Roleplay Platform

NexTavern (formerly SillyTavern Lite / Tavern Lite) is our standalone AI roleplay platform. Clean, easy to use, and focused on letting you create characters quickly and engage in immersive AI conversations with a variety of gameplay modes.

Core Features

  1. Character Creation & Management

    • In the Workshop, you can define your character’s appearance, personality, backstory, and dialogue style in detail.
    • In the Square, browse, search, and manage all your characters.

  2. Unique Game Modes

    • Free Chat: Have open-ended one-on-one conversations with your characters.
    • Challenge Mode: Set a clear goal (e.g., “Earn their trust”) and navigate your conversations to achieve it.
    • DnD Mode: Experience full tabletop roleplaying rules — create character sheets, use attributes and dice rolls to determine the outcome of your adventures.
    • Group Chat Mode: Bring multiple AI characters into one conversation and watch — or join — their lively interactions.

  3. Complete Conversation Experience

    • All conversations are auto-saved in your Session history, ready to revisit, manage, or export at any time.
    • Adjust the AI’s creativity level to make responses more stable or more surprising.

How to Get Started with NexTavern

  1. Visit the platform: Click the button above or go to NexTavern .
  2. Configure AI Service: In Session → Config, enter your AI API Key (supports DeepSeek, OpenAI, and more). This is the engine behind the character conversations.
  3. Create or Import a Character: Build your first character in the Workshop, or import an existing character card from the community.
  4. Start Chatting: Select a character and begin. Start with the classic free mode, then gradually explore Challenge Mode or DnD Mode for deeper gameplay.

Important Notes

  • Data Storage: Your character data and conversation history are stored locally in your browser. Please back up important content regularly using the export feature.
  • Bring Your Own API Key: You’ll need to provide your own AI service API key to use all features. Detailed setup guides are available.
  • Get Help: If you encounter any issues, feel free to join the player community below.

Our Design Philosophy

NEXWORLD is more than a game — it is an exploration of narrative possibilities.

Our core mission is to create the ultimate freedom and narrative depth. Unlike traditional games, there is no single script here. You face a dynamically responsive world where every interaction subtly shifts the story’s direction. We are committed to making this freedom a foundation for coherent, unique stories — not a source of chaos — through carefully designed rules.

AI technology is the key tool that brings this vision to life. In NEXWORLD, AI breathes vitality into the world, intelligently generating fitting content and challenges based on your actions, ensuring every adventure feels authentic and unique. Technology serves the experience; everything is in service of great gameplay.

In terms of presentation, we believe in “text first, visuals second.” Text has an irreplaceable power to spark imagination, while carefully chosen images enhance immersion and outline the world. We strive for a clean, textured overall experience.

The NEXWORLD project is still in active development. With a passion for narrative and game design, we invite you to join this experiment — share your feelings and ideas, and help shape the future of this world.
